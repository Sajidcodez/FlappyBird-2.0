Flappy Bird

How to Play

Objective: Guide the bird through the pipes without hitting them.

Controls: Press SPACEBAR to make the bird jump and flap its wings.

Scoring: Earn 1 point for each pipe you pass through.

Game Modes:

  • Easy: Slower game speed with longer delays between pipes
  • Medium: Normal game speed
  • Hard: Faster game speed with shorter delays between pipes
  • Infinite: Play indefinitely with no stages or win condition. Set the highest score possible!

Stages (Regular Modes Only):

  • Stage 1: 0-24 points
  • Stage 2: 25-49 points
  • Stage 3: 50-74 points
  • Stage 4: 75-99 points
  • Stage 5: 100 points - YOU WIN!

Score Tracking: Your scores are automatically saved and displayed in the "View Scores" section. Top 10 scores are kept with timestamps.

Tips: Time your jumps carefully and stay centered between the pipes! In Infinite mode, aim for the highest score possible!
